Compliancy Details

If you’re handling protected health information, your technological resources need to be HIPAA compliant.Cybersecurity attacks on healthcare providers are getting worse- 2016 alone has seen 2 million people impacted from a reported 49 breaches of U.S. medical data, ranging from Hollywood Presbyterian Medical Center’s $3.6 million ransomware attack in February to the Central Ohio Urology Group’s loss of 150 GB worth of patient records and financial information in early August.

The frequent compliance audits and risk assessments required by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act are meant to ensure your processes, from EHR Management to cloud services to your office’s hardware, are keeping your patient’s data safe and private. That’s why at ulltium consulting, we take your security as seriously as you take your patient’s health. All of our solutions are compliant with HIPAA guidelines, and our routine risk assessments help us thoroughly identify any vulnerabilities within your organization. The policies, controls, and technical safeguards we put in place, along with their enforcement and any necessary training, helps ensure both your technology and the people using it aren’t putting you or your patients at risk.
